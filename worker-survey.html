Research Information Sheet গবেষণা তথ্যপত্র

You are invited to participate in a study: "Workplace Illness Trends in the Bangladesh RMG Sector" — a MBBC research programme.


Purpose: To understand occupational illness types, frequency, and risk factors among RMG workers and their impact on productivity.


Confidentiality: Your identity remains completely anonymous. No names or factory names will be published.


Voluntariness: Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without consequence.


Duration: Approximately 12–18 minutes.
